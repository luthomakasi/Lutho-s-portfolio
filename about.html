About Me

Hello, my name is Lutho Makasi, and I am currently pursuing an advanced diploma in Computer Science at Tshwane University of Technology. I hold a Diploma in ICT with a specialization in Applications Development.

In my professional journey, I am gaining hands-on experience as an Intern Systems Developer at Capaciti, where I focus on the creation of 3-tier applications. My technical expertise includes working with a variety of technologies such as Java, HTML, CSS, Java EE, SQL, Spring, Bootstrap, Git, and Spring Boot.

I am passionate about leveraging my skills in application development to contribute meaningfully to the technology field. My current focus on 3-tier applications reflects my dedication to creating robust and scalable solutions. I find great joy in keeping up with industry trends and continuously expanding my knowledge.

Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I am driven by a commitment to excellence and a curiosity that fuels my continuous learning. I am excited about the opportunity to bring my skills and enthusiasm for technology to contribute to innovative projects

Notably, I secured the third place in one of the hackathons, demonstrating my ability to contribute effectively in competitive environments.
